# Stale USB toast hijack

Rank: 02 (round-3 kalite/bug brainstorm, 24->23->10, satir-numarali dogrulanmis)

BUG (M). MainForm tek NotifyIcon balonu + tek OnBalloonClicked'i USB/tehdit/ozet toast'lari icin paylasiyor; handler once _pendingUsbDrive'a bakiyor.
