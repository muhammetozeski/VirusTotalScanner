# Flag double-extension lure files from the watcher as an urgent, distinct alert

Kategori: protection | Efor: S | Rank: 1

## Ne
In DownloadsWatcher.VerdictOne (DownloadsWatcher.cs:96) detect the masquerade pattern where Path.GetFileNameWithoutExtension itself ends in a document/media extension while the real extension is exec-class (invoice.pdf.exe, photo.jpg.scr, setup.docx.bat). On a match set ScanItem.OriginNote='cift uzanti tuzagi' and raise ThreatFound even when the hash comes back clean/unknown, routing it through OnThreatFound so it surfaces as a warning toast (OriginSuffix already renders OriginNote at MainForm.cs:376) instead of being silently Cleared as just another unknown exe.

## Neden
Double-extension files are a top social-engineering trick, dangerous by their naming alone and independent of whether VirusTotal has the exact hash, so they catch brand-new payloads no engine has cataloged. No filename-masquerade heuristic exists anywhere today (VerdictOne currently only does trust->cache->keyless). Pure offline, zero-quota, fully inside the existing watcher path.
