# Replace the O(n^2) chip-count rescan with incremental bucket counters

Kategori: perf | Efor: M | Rank: 5

## Ne
UpdateChipCounts (ScanQueueControl.cs:268-290) loops every item in _scheduler.Items to recompute six bucket totals, and it is called both from the 250ms _repaintTimer (line 137) AND from OnFilterItemFinished (line 298) on EVERY ItemFinished — O(n) per completion, O(n^2) across a scan, on the UI thread. Keep six int fields and update them on each item's bucket transition (the final bucket is known when Report/Status is set), so UpdateChipCounts just reads six ints; let the 250ms timer be the only thing that pushes the labels and drop the per-ItemFinished UpdateChipCounts call. Also replace the linear _view.Contains(item) at line 299 with a HashSet<ScanItem> membership set kept alongside _view.

## Neden
On a 50k-file scan the current full-rescan path does on the order of a billion item touches on the UI thread, directly causing chip labels to lag and the grid to stutter late in a big scan. Constant-time updates keep the chips and grid smooth regardless of queue size.
