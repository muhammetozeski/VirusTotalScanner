# Threat Family Clusters - cross-scan correlation

A local Family Clusters view joining ALL cache.json entries by the stored normalized Family token (ThreatLabel + MajorFlaggers secondary). Emit clusters of 2+ DISTINCT hashes that resolve to the same family; each row pivots member count, distinct LastPath locations, combined first/last-seen span, detection range, shared label. One click fans all member hashes into CopyFinderService (find every live copy); one click adds the cluster to the re-check sweep. Pure in-memory group-by over cache.json (reuses Family/FamilyCount/ThreatLabel/MajorFlaggers/LastPath). Catches polymorphic campaigns (fresh hash each time). Zero quota.
