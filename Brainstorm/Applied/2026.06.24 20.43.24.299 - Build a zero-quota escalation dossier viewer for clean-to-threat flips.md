# Build a zero-quota escalation dossier viewer for clean-to-threat flips

Kategori: protection | Efor: M | Rank: 12

## Ne
New EscalationDossierDialog.cs opened from the red banner in ScanHistoryControl.RefreshEscBanner. Today that banner's only action (OpenReverdict) opens HistoryReverdictDialog, which RE-RUNS live VT queries against every once-cleared file. The dossier instead just READS the durable EscalationStore.All() records (EscalationStore.cs:39, no VT calls): one timeline card per EscalationRecord showing Name, FirstScanUtc->FlipUtc, OldRatio->NewRatio (already exposed at lines 15-20), days-clean-before-flip, and current on-disk presence via File.Exists on the path resolved by joining Hash to ScanHistoryStore by Sha256. Per-row actions: Quarantine / Open location / Copy SHA-256 / Open on VT.

## Neden
The highest-value signal the app produces ('a file I cleared later went bad') is persisted in escalations.json but only surfaced as a count in a banner that opens a quota-spending re-query, not a view of the flips themselves. This turns the durable ledger into an actionable, quota-free list: which files flipped, when, and whether they are still on disk to neutralize now.
