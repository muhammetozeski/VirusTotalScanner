# Show scan recency on the overview with a staleness nudge

Kategori: ux | Efor: S | Rank: 1

## Ne
In ScanOverviewControl.Refresh2 (lines 150-179), take the max HistoryEntry.WhenLocal/WhenUtc from ScanHistoryStore.All() and render a recency line near the count tiles: 'Son tarama: bugun / 3 gun once / hic'. When the newest scan is older than Settings.RecheckPeriodDays, tint it amber and push a message into the existing UpdateAttention list (lines 282-298) nudging the user to run 'Indirilenleri tara' or 'Yeniden denetle'. Pure read-side; no quota.

## Neden
The three tiles are lifetime totals (Refresh2 counts every entry ever), so a user seeing '0 Tehdit' cannot tell 'clean today' from 'haven't scanned in two months'. A plain recency line turns the overview into an honest at-a-glance answer and prompts a re-scan before a stale clean verdict gives false comfort. Data already in history.json.
